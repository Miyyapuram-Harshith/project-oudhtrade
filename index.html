Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about trading Agarwood on OudhTrade.

What is OudhTrade and who is it for? ＋

OudhTrade is a global directory registry connecting certified buyers, sellers, nurseries, farmers, and inoculation specialists dealing in Agarwood and Oud essential oils. It is built to facilitate legally compliant negotiations.

What is CITES Appendix II compliance? ＋

All species of the genus Aquilaria and Gyrinops (which produce Agarwood) are listed under CITES Appendix II. This means their international trade is regulated to prevent endangerment, requiring exporters to provide government-issued CITES certificates. OudhTrade uses AI to audit listings for compliance and asks sellers to upload documentation.

How does Symmetric Contact work? ＋

Symmetric Contact ensures that any email-verified platform member can directly open messaging threads and initiate contact with any other member. Role switching is disabled in OudhTrade to maintain profile credibility (a Buyer cannot switch to a Seller, and vice-versa).

Are there transaction fees on OudhTrade? ＋

No. OudhTrade is a listing-only directory platform. We do not process payments or escrow. All transactions, shipping, and CITES permit handling are negotiated directly between the buyers and sellers via our integrated secure messaging system.
